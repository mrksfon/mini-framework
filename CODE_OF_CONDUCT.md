# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus discussions on ideas and technical decisions.
- Assume good intentions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing another person's private information
- Disruptive or intentionally hostile conduct

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the repository maintainer through the contact information on their GitHub profile.

Reports will be reviewed fairly and handled confidentially.
